How to read these fields
Field Dataset-aligned meaning
Monthly premium The monthly amount listed for the plan. Annual premium shown above is monthly premium multiplied by
12.
Annual deductible The annual deductible value supplied for the plan. The dataset does not define separate
individual/family or in-/out-of-network deductibles.
Copay percentage Stored in the source as copay_pct. This document treats it as the plan's stated member cost-share
percentage without inventing service-specific copays.
Coverage type / The categorical values supplied in plans.csv. Referral, pharmacy, out-of-network, and authorization rules
network tier are not provided.
